Welcome to the DePew Cafe Official Website

DePew's Cafe is all about an honest living and with the soul that is put into our food and customer service. We're all smiles and high fives around here.

DePew's Cafe started out as a small restaurant on the corner that everyone talked about. Back in 2000 there was a boy named Edward DePew who wanted to serve his food to people all around the world. He though he was such a talented cook that people deserved to try some of his food. He had crazy ideas from chocolate spaghetti, to purple fried pickles. Who knew that one day his dream of serving others would come to life.
